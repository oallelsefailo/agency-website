# Vysible Marketing Site Brief

## Product Summary

Vysible is a white-label ecommerce analytics dashboard for Magento 2 merchants. It pulls Google Analytics 4 traffic, Google Search Console search visibility, and Magento order data into one hosted executive operating view. The product is built for agency delivery: it can run instantly in demo mode with no client credentials, then switch to live data through configuration once a merchant is ready.

The dashboard is not positioned as another BI tool. It is a focused operating layer for ecommerce leaders who need to know what changed, what deserves attention, and where revenue, search, and merchandising signals intersect. The core promise is practical visibility: fewer disconnected reports, fewer spreadsheet rituals, and faster leadership reviews.

## Target Audience

Primary buyers:

- Ecommerce agencies serving Magento 2 merchants who want a branded analytics product they can package into a monthly retainer.
- Magento operators, founders, and marketing leads who need a clean executive dashboard without standing up heavy BI infrastructure.
- Technical consultants and fractional ecommerce leads who need a repeatable analytics walkthrough for prospects and clients.

Secondary influencers:

- SEO and performance marketing teams who need Search Console and GA4 context in the same view as revenue.
- Merchandising teams who need product, category, and order movement surfaced without waiting on custom reporting.
- Client stakeholders who need a boardroom-ready view they can understand without learning analytics tools.

## Key Differentiators

- Unified ecommerce signal: GA4 revenue and traffic, Search Console visibility, and Magento order-line data appear in one executive view.
- AI-assisted operating briefs: Vysible translates source-grounded metrics into concise review notes, evidence, and next-review prompts.
- Priority review targets: the product identifies concrete pages, products, and categories worth operator attention instead of stopping at charts.
- Demo mode by default: agencies can show the product immediately, before a merchant has granted access or completed onboarding.
- White-label delivery: client name, brand treatment, and hosted dashboard experience can be configured for agency-led service models.
- Magento-specific focus: the product speaks the language of ecommerce operations, merchandising movement, low-CTR collections, order-line revenue, and category performance.
- Lightweight deployment: Vysible avoids the cost, complexity, and maintenance burden of bloated BI stacks.

## Tone Of Voice

Write like a technical founder talking to ecommerce operators. The copy should be direct, specific, and operational. Avoid generic SaaS claims such as "boost growth," "unlock insights," or "supercharge your business." Prefer claims that name the data source, workflow, or decision being improved.

Voice principles:

- Specific over inflated: "Review low-CTR collection pages before changing campaign spend" beats "make smarter marketing decisions."
- Operator-first: name the weekly review, client walkthrough, merchandising check, and executive readout.
- Calm confidence: premium and technical, not breathless.
- Transparent: explain that Vysible is a hosted, white-label service with demo mode and live-data configuration.
- Evidence-led: emphasize source-grounded metrics, connected systems, and review targets.

Sample copy direction:

- "A white-label operating view for Magento revenue, search visibility, and merchandising signals."
- "Show the demo before credentials exist. Switch to live data when the client is ready."
- "Turn GA4, Search Console, and Magento order data into the review queue your client actually needs."
- "Built for agencies that want analytics as a retained service, not a one-off report."

## Page Structure Recommendation

### Landing Page

Goal: Convert agency and merchant visitors into demo views and contact leads.

Recommended flow:

1. Sticky navigation with Vysible logo, product/pricing/contact links, theme toggle, and persistent "See Demo" CTA.
2. Full-viewport hero with a dark, premium visual system, founder-grade headline, concise subheadline, "See Demo" and "Contact Us" CTAs, and the refreshed executive overview screenshot in a browser mockup.
3. Source strip showing GA4, Search Console, Magento, and AI Brief as connected signals.
4. Product proof section using scroll-revealed screenshots: Executive Overview, Executive Brief, Priority Review Targets.
5. Six feature cards: unified executive view, AI brief, priority targets, Search Console intelligence, merchandising movement, white-label demo-to-live delivery.
6. How it works: Connect sources, configure the brand/workspace, go live with the hosted dashboard.
7. Social proof placeholders written as realistic agency/operator quotes.
8. Pricing preview with Starter, Growth, and Agency tiers, highlighting Growth.
9. Final CTA focused on booking a walkthrough or opening the live demo.

### Demo Page

Goal: Route visitors to the live demo subdomain or provide a clear launch CTA.

Recommended content:

- Minimal redirect-style page.
- Reassure that demo mode requires no credentials.
- Include fallback link if automatic redirect is not implemented.

### Pricing Page

Goal: Make the business model legible and retainer-friendly.

Recommended content:

- Three tiers: Starter, Growth, Agency.
- Feature comparison table with source connections, branding, briefs, review targets, support level, and dashboard count.
- Clear note that exact implementation scope can vary by Magento setup and agency package.

### Contact Page

Goal: Collect qualified agency/merchant leads.

Recommended fields:

- Name, email, company, website, role, monthly order volume, interested tier, message.
- Formspree or Netlify Forms integration.
- Side panel explaining ideal-fit customers and expected response.

### Privacy Page

Goal: Establish trust for analytics and AI workflows.

Recommended content:

- Collected data: name, email, analytics data, Search Console data, Magento order data, configuration details.
- Usage: service delivery, dashboard operation, support, communication, product improvement.
- Third parties: Google, OpenAI, hosting/provider tools.
- Retention, deletion, client data ownership, and contact at `vysiblesales@gmail.com`.

### Terms Page

Goal: Set commercial and operational expectations.

Recommended content:

- Service description, payment terms, cancellation, acceptable use, client data ownership, limitations of liability, governing law in Southern California.

## Positioning Summary

Vysible should be positioned as an ecommerce operating dashboard agencies can sell, brand, and run for Magento 2 clients. The website should make the product feel more focused than BI, more useful than a reporting dashboard, and easier to adopt than a custom analytics project.
