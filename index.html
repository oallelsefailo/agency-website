How it works

No reporting rebuild. No new stack. Just your existing sources, connected.

01

You share access, we handle the rest

Add your Google account as a Viewer on GA4 and Search Console. Run one SQL command to create a read-only Magento database user. That's the entire setup ask on your end.

02

We connect and configure

We wire GA4, Search Console, and Magento order data into your dashboard, validate the numbers against your admin reports, and flip it from demo data to live sources.

03

Open it instead of building it

Executive briefs, revenue movement, SEO priorities, and a weekly review queue ready the next time you or your client needs a read on what's happening.
