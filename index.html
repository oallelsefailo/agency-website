Dashboard overview

The full picture before you dig into anything specific.

Traffic, revenue, conversion, and search performance alongside your live Magento order data. The overview gives you the shape of the business at a glance. No tab switching, no mental overhead assembling context from parts.

Date ranges are selectable. Periods compare automatically. Everything in one viewport.

AI executive briefs

The brief that was always supposed to exist.

Each dashboard load generates a plain-English summary of what is moving: where traffic came from, what revenue did, which categories performed. Written from your actual data, not a template.

No customer names, no order details leave the dashboard. Aggregated metrics only. If the AI is unavailable, a rule-based version runs from the same data. You always get an output.

Priority review targets

What to look at. Not a list of everything.

The review targets surface specific items worth attention this period. A page with high impressions but low CTR. A product with a revenue drop. A category outperforming the rest.

Six to twelve targets depending on your plan, each pulled from actual signal in the data. Not a fixed checklist. Not generic suggestions.
