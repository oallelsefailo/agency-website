AI Chat is a built-in chat interface that lets you ask plain questions about your Magento store and get answers pulled directly from your live database. Ask about revenue, orders, products, or categories without writing SQL or exporting data. Available on Growth and Agency plans. Sessions are not saved and reset on refresh.

The AI powers two things in your dashboard: the Executive Brief and the Priority Review Targets. For both, it only sees aggregated metrics for the selected period. Things like total sessions, revenue, conversion rate, search impressions, top pages, and product revenue movement. No customer names, no order details, no personally identifiable information ever leaves your dashboard.

The Executive Brief takes those numbers and writes a plain English summary of where things stand and what's moving. The Priority Review Targets use the same data to surface specific areas worth a closer look, whether that's a page with high impressions and low CTR, a product with dropping revenue, or a category that's outperforming everything else.

If the AI is unavailable for any reason, both fall back to a rule-based version built from the same data so you always get an output either way.

OpenAI's API does not train on API inputs so your data is never used to improve their models.
