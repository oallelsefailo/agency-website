Privacy Policy

This policy explains how Vysible handles contact information, connected analytics data, and service providers while operating white-label ecommerce dashboards.

OverviewData we collectHow we use dataGoogle, OpenAI, and providersRetention and deletionSecurityCalifornia privacy rightsClient data ownershipContact

Overview

Vysible provides hosted ecommerce analytics dashboards for Magento 2 merchants and agencies. We collect and process information only as needed to deliver the dashboard, communicate with customers, support the service, and improve the product experience.

Data we collect

We may collect name, email address, company details, website URL, message content, configuration details, Google Analytics 4 data, Google Search Console data, Magento order data, and dashboard usage information. Magento order data may include order-line revenue, product, category, and transaction-level reporting fields needed for analytics.

How we use data

We use collected data for service delivery, dashboard operation, customer support, sales communication, source connection troubleshooting, AI executive brief generation, priority review target generation, security monitoring, and product improvement.

Google, OpenAI, and service providers

Vysible uses the following service providers to operate the service: Google (GA4 and Search Console data connection), OpenAI (AI executive brief and review target generation), Hetzner (hosting infrastructure), Cloudflare (network security, DNS, and demo access authentication), and Web3Forms (contact form processing). Cloudflare processes email addresses entered during demo access authentication. These providers process information only as needed to support Vysible operations and do not receive data beyond what is required for their specific function. OpenAI does not train on API inputs submitted through Vysible.

Data retention and deletion

We retain customer contact information and dashboard configuration details for as long as needed to provide the service. GA4, Search Console, and Magento source data is queried on demand when the dashboard loads and is not stored on Vysible infrastructure. No customer order records, transaction details, or personally identifiable information from connected sources is retained. Customers may request deletion of their contact and configuration data by contacting Vysible at vysiblesales@gmail.com.

Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect customer information. No internet service is perfectly secure, and customers should maintain appropriate access controls for their Google, Magento, and related accounts. The Vysible marketing site may use software to measure traffic and improve the product. No personally identifiable information is collected.

California Privacy Rights

California residents may request access to, correction of, or deletion of their personal information by contacting Vysible at vysiblesales@gmail.com. Vysible does not sell personal information. Vysible does not share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising. California residents will not be discriminated against for exercising their privacy rights.

Client data ownership

Clients retain ownership of their source data. Vysible uses connected data to provide dashboards, briefs, review targets, support, and related service functions. Vysible does not sell client analytics data.

Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy or data handling can be sent to Vysible at vysiblesales@gmail.com.
