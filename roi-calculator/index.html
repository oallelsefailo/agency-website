What is manual reporting actually costing you?

Your tools are free, and you will still open GA4, Search Console, and the Magento admin for the deep dives. What costs money is the repetitive part: pulling the same numbers out of all three every week and reconciling them into one view for your review. That recurring assembly is the job Vysible removes, not the analysis itself. Put in your own numbers below.

Nothing here is guessed, the hours are yours. We turn your weekly hours into a monthly number (a month is about 4.3 weeks), then multiply by your hourly rate and the number of stores. This counts only the recurring time spent combining the sources, not the deep analysis you would still do inside the tools.

That time goes away when the sources are already connected.

Vysible pulls GA4, Search Console, and your Magento data into one dashboard, with a plain English brief on what changed and where to look.
